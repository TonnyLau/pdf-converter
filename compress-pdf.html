What is PDFSwitch?

PDFSwitch is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, PDFSwitch offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Compress PDF tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Compress as many PDFs as you need without any cost or restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • Effective Compression: Significantly reduce your PDF file size while maintaining the best possible quality.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • Batch Processing: Compress multiple PDF files at once, saving you time and effort.

How to use the Compress PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF files onto the upload area, or click to select them from your device.
  2. Step 2: Click the "Process Files" button to start the compression.
  3. Step 3: Once processed, download your smaller, compressed PDF file(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it safe to compress my PDF files here?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device. We do not upload or store any of your documents, ensuring complete privacy.

How much will my file be compressed?

The compression level depends on the content of your PDF. Files with many images will see a more significant size reduction than text-only documents. Our tool aims to provide the best balance between file size and quality.

Does compressing a PDF reduce its quality?

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce file size with minimal impact on quality. For most documents, any quality change is unnoticeable, especially for text. Image quality is slightly reduced to achieve smaller file sizes, but it is optimized for on-screen viewing.
