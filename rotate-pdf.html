What is 全能PDF?

全能PDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, 全能PDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Rotate PDF Pages tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Rotate as many PDFs as you need without any cost or restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • Flexible Rotation Options: Rotate all pages in your PDF by 90°, 180°, or 270° clockwise to correct their orientation.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • Fast and Efficient: Quickly rotate your PDF pages and get the corrected file in seconds.

How to use the Rotate PDF Pages tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF file onto the upload area, or click to select it from your device.
  2. Step 2: Select the desired rotation angle (90°, 180°, or 270°) from the dropdown menu.
  3. Step 3: Click the "Process Files" button to start the rotation.
  4. Step 4: Once processed, download your new, rotated PDF from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I rotate individual pages to different angles?

Currently, this tool rotates all pages in the document by the same selected angle. For more advanced page manipulation, you might need to use a combination of our 'Split PDF' and 'Merge PDF' tools.

Will rotating the PDF reduce its quality?

No, rotating a PDF is a lossless operation. The quality of your text and images will remain exactly the same as in the original document.

Is it safe to rotate my sensitive documents here?

Yes. All processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device. We do not upload or store any of your documents, ensuring complete privacy.
