What is PDFSwitch?

PDFSwitch is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, PDFSwitch offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Extract PDF Pages tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Extract pages from as many PDFs as you need without any cost or restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • Precise Page Selection: Easily specify individual pages or ranges to create a new PDF with only the content you need.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • Fast and Efficient: Quickly extract pages without waiting for server processing.

How to use the Extract PDF Pages tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF file onto the upload area, or click to select it from your device.
  2. Step 2: In the options box, enter the page numbers or ranges you want to extract (e.g., 1, 3, 5-8).
  3. Step 3: Click the "Process Files" button to start the extraction.
  4. Step 4: Once processed, download your new PDF containing only the extracted pages from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it safe to extract pages from my PDF here?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device. We do not upload or store any of your documents, ensuring complete privacy.

How do I specify the pages I want to extract?

You can enter page numbers separated by commas (e.g., 1, 3, 10) for individual pages, or use a hyphen for a range of pages (e.g., 5-8). You can also combine them (e.g., 1, 3, 5-8).

What's the difference between Extract Pages and Split PDF?

The Extract Pages tool creates a single new PDF file containing only the pages you specified. The Split PDF tool, on the other hand, is designed to break a PDF into multiple smaller files based on page ranges.
