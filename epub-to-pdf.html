What is PDFSwitch?

PDFSwitch is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. Every conversion runs in your browser so your files stay on your device. No ads, no registration, no upload queue, and no limits.

Why use our EPUB to PDF tool?

  • High-Fidelity Conversion: PDFSwitch reads the EPUB package, inlines its embedded CSS, fonts, and images, then renders chapters into a high-resolution PDF.
  • Private by Default: Your ebook never leaves your device. The conversion is fully client-side.
  • Preserves Visual Quality: Chapter styling, illustrations, typography, and spacing are retained much more faithfully than plain-text export pipelines.
  • Smooth for Longer Books: Chapters are rendered one by one before being paginated into the final PDF, which keeps conversion more stable for larger EPUB files.
  • Free and Unlimited: No paywall, no account, no usage cap.

How to use the EPUB to PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop one or more `.epub` files into the upload area, or click to choose them from your device.
  2. Step 2: Click Process Files to begin the conversion.
  3. Step 3: Wait for PDFSwitch to render each chapter and build the final PDF.
  4. Step 4: Download the finished PDF file from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my EPUB files uploaded to your servers?

No. EPUB conversion happens locally in your browser. PDFSwitch does not upload, inspect, or store your ebook files.

Will the EPUB formatting be preserved in the PDF?

The tool is designed to preserve embedded chapter styling, images, fonts, and layout as closely as possible. Results are typically much closer to the original EPUB than a plain-text conversion.

Can I convert multiple EPUB files at once?

Yes. Add several EPUB files and PDFSwitch will generate a separate PDF for each one.

Does this work with image-heavy or styled EPUB books?

Yes. PDFSwitch specifically inlines embedded stylesheets, illustrations, and fonts from the EPUB archive so styled books convert with better visual fidelity.
