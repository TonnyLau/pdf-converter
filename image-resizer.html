What is PDFSwitch?

PDFSwitch is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, PDFSwitch offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Image Resizer tool?

  • 100% Private: All resizing happens locally in your browser, so your files never leave your device.
  • Multiple Resize Modes: Resize by percentage, fixed resolution with locked aspect ratio, or free width and height control.
  • Popular Image Support: Resize JPEG, PNG, and WEBP files with one tool.
  • Batch Friendly: Upload and resize multiple images in one run.
  • Free and Unlimited: No account, no payment, and no usage caps.

How to use the Image Resizer tool?

  1. Upload images by dragging and dropping files, or click the upload area to choose files.
  2. Pick your resize mode: percentage, locked aspect ratio, or free width and height.
  3. If needed, remove any uploaded image with the remove button before processing.
  4. Click Resize Image to process your files.
  5. Download each resized image and review the file size and resolution details shown in results.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my images uploaded to your servers?

No. Everything is processed client-side in your browser and never uploaded.

Which image formats are supported?

This tool supports JPEG, PNG, and WEBP image files.

Can I keep aspect ratio while changing resolution?

Yes. Choose the locked aspect ratio mode and width/height will stay proportional automatically.

Can I control width and height independently?

Yes. Choose the free resolution mode to set width and height independently.
