What is PDFOnly?

PDFOnly is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, PDFOnly offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our PDF Encryption tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Protect as many PDFs as you want.
  • Privacy First: Encryption happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
  • Open Source: Verify our privacy claims and contribute.
  • Strong Encryption: AES-256 available for maximum security.
  • No Registration Required: Use instantly.

How to add a password to your PDF:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF into the upload area, or click to select it.
  2. Step 2: Enter and confirm the new password in the options box.
  3. Step 3: Click "Process Files" to encrypt your PDF.
  4. Step 4: Download your new, password-protected PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is encryption done on your servers?

No. Everything happens in your browser. Your files are never uploaded, ensuring complete privacy.

What encryption strength is used?

We support strong AES-256 encryption, offering robust protection for your PDFs.

Can I remove a password later?

Yes—use our Remove PDF Password tool to decrypt PDFs you have access to.
