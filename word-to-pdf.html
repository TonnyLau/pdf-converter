What is PDFSwitch?

PDFSwitch is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, PDFSwitch offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our DOCX to PDF tool?

  • High Fidelity Rendering: Uses a DOCX page renderer designed to preserve Word layouts, tables, colors, spacing, and typography.
  • 100% Private: Runs entirely in your browser. Files never leave your device.
  • Free & Open Source: No registration, ads, or limits.
  • Complex Document Support: Better handling for headers, footers, footnotes, tables, and styled content.
  • Batch Conversion: Convert multiple DOCX files in one go.

How to use the Word to PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your .docx files into the upload area, or click to select them.
  2. Step 2: Click the "Process Files" button to start the conversion.
  3. Step 3: Once processed, download your new PDF(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my Word files uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Will the formatting be preserved?

We now use a higher-fidelity DOCX rendering pipeline for better visual matching to the original Word file, including complex formatting. Some edge-case fonts or unsupported Word features may still vary slightly by browser.

Can I convert multiple DOCX files at once?

Yes, batch conversion is supported. Add multiple files before processing.
